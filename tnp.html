Training & Placement
Industry Institute relationship:
Association of Engineers, India, is closely associated with industry for grooming students up to the Industry Standard.
The salient features of our processes are:
  • Yearly Industries visit and meet with industry peoples.
  • Every year at the end of 3rd Semester Industry experts from various companies meet with our students and exchange ideas and help them employability. Since 2014 AEI has hosted Industry meet with active participation from students to develop our students for the professional world.
  • At regular interval AEI conduct workshops, seminar, panel discussion, talk from experts, technical laboratory visits, visit to Port/Oil refinery/ship- building company or other reputed company under direct supervision of our faculty.
  • Training and evaluation through class test, practice session, group discussion is a regular feature of students as a part of their curriculum. Recently we have started soft skill development class under a professional guidance and advice of experienced teacher.
  • The continuous relationship with Regional Labour Institute and Institute Environment and Hygiene, Kolkata enriched knowledge of our students with lab facilities and technical requirement.
